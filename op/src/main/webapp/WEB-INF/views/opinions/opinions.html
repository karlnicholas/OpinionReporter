Welcome to the California Court Opinions Website.

This site reads and parses recently published California Appellate Court Cases. The California statutes that each case references are shown when you click on the case title.

Use the "Dates" tab above to view cases currently published at the California Judicial Council Website.

You can sign-up with your e-mail under the "User" tab. With your own account you can filter which cases you see. For example, if you only want to see cases that reference the Business & Professions code, you can filter for that. As a bonus, when you sign up, you get weekly email updates listing the cases you're interested in.

The parsing algorithm does a good job, but it is not perfect. Occasionally code references are left out or wrongly included. The parsing will give you a general idea of what the case is about, but if you really want to know what the case is about you should click on the case name and read the court's opinion.

Court Opinions welcomes your feedback, which may be submitted under the "Applications" tab. Feel free to share this website with your fellow attorneys and friends.
